## 2. Description of Service

Rack Builder is a web-based reference tool designed to assist USMC military personnel in understanding and building compliant awards and ribbon racks in accordance with:
- MCO 1020.34H (Marine Corps Order)
- SECNAVINST 1650.1J (Secretary of Navy Instruction)
- SECNAV M-1650.1 (Secretary of Navy Memorandum)

The App provides:
- A searchable library of USMC-applicable awards and decorations.
- A visual rack preview based on user input.
- Regulatory citations and criteria for each award.
- Device (star, oak leaf, numeral) configuration guidance.
